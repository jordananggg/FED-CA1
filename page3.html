Sustainability in SP

Singapore Polytechnic has done many things to ensure sustainability within its campus. The following are a few measures that SP has taken to do so!

1. Having Many Water Dispensers

Did you know that there are over 110 water dispensers located across SP? Having these water dispensers help cultivate eco-conscious habits by encouraging students and staff to use water dispensers around campus. This also allows SP to stop providing bottled water at events, greatly decreasing waste produced!

2. Diversity of Flora

There are currently 230 of flora which are thriving in SP, including Brown Woolly Fig Heritage Trees found near Food Court 1! This diversity in plants not only allows for the preservation of these species, carbon dioxide levels in the area could decrease due to the sheer abundance of plants!

3. Green Mark Platinum Buildings

Singapore Polytechnic currently has 4 buildings which are Green Mark Platinum certified, with aims to certify all 22 buildings on campus by 2025! By having the Green Mark Platinum certification, it shows that the building is more environmentally friendly as it uses less water, electricity & resources
